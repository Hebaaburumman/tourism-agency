Jordan

Why should you visit Jordan?

Jordan, an Arab country on the Jordan River's east bank, is distinguished by ancient monuments, nature reserves, and seaside resorts. It is the location of the famous archaeological site of Petra, the Nabatean capital from around 300 B.C.


Don't miss the amazing experience of floating in Jordan's Dead Sea, visiting Aqaba for diving in Jordan, Ajloun Castle, visiting Jerash, visiting Petra, and visiting Wadi Rum. These are just a few of the things you can do while in Jordan.
