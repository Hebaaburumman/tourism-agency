About Us

Welcome to Al-Bosta, the premier tourism agency dedicated to enhancing your journey to Jordan and Palestine. We are passionate about immersing you in the vibrant cultures, breathtaking views, and invigorating experiences that these destinations have to offer. Get ready to embark on an extraordinary adventure and create unforgettable memories.

What is Al-Bosta measns ?

The idea behind Al-Bosta from the nostalgia and positive memories associated with the buses that used to traverse the Sham region. These buses were once a symbol of freedom, connecting people as they embarked on journeys to visit loved ones, explore new destinations, and create cherished memories.

Traditionally, many individuals would travel between the Sham region, Jordan, and Palestine using these buses to reach the breathtaking mountains and expansive beaches that dot the region. However, over time, these buses became predominantly associated with the transportation of Palestinian prisoners, which brought about a negative connotation to these once prospering buses that carried a sense of unity.

To reclaim the true essence and spirit of Al-Bosta, we established this travel agency. Our mission is to revitalize the concept of these buses and restore their original meaning – a symbol of unity, exploration, and the beauty of Middle Eastern culture. We aim to offer travelers a chance to experience the wonders of Jordan and Palestine, to immerse themselves in the hidden gems, ancient sites, and natural wonders that make Jordan and Palestine so captivating.
